Shiny New Macbook Pro

My current MacBook Pro has been a warrior. It’s a 2010 model, with a core 2 duo processor and 8GB of RAM. It has a SSD for the system drive (which occupies the optical drive space) and a 500GB 7200RPM drive where the regular system drive goes. Sadly, it’s been through some rough times, include a 2 year period of just not working. It’s started disconnecting the 500GB Media Drive, the trackpad isn’t working as well, and Vagrant or any VM are nightmareisly slow. It’s time for a shiny new MacBook Pro.
