About Me

Hi there! My name is Kris Brandow, but around most of the internet I’m skriptble.

I’m a Full Stack Engineer for Columbia University Medical College and I love building stuff using web technologies. I develop in PHP and other web technologies like Javascript, MySQL, MongoDB, Drupal, Node.js, HTML, and CSS. I’m a full stack developer, so I’ll work on anything, from bear metal to front end.

My IDE & text editor of choice is VIM, some people love full blown IDEs and some love text editors. I love VIM. It gives me a ton of flexibility, comes with almost any linux distro, and with a little configuration is becomes extremely powerful. That said, when I’m not doing something on the command line I also use MacVim or TextWrangler.

I enjoy learning and reading. If you see me on the subway, I’ll probably have some headphones in either listening to a podcast or reading a book. My fascination jumps around, a side effect of being a developer of the entire stack. I’ll research anything that will help expand my knowledge of how to engineer software. I especially am interested in theory relating to productivity and solving complex problems, and I enjoy learning about new programming languages and databases.

My major in college was writing. As such I’m fond of writing outside of code. I mostly write fiction, but I also know how to write screenplays and stage plays. By far I write more code than either of these I try hard to ensure my code is either throughly commented or straight forward enough to understand from reading it.
